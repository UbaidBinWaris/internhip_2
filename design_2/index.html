About PPMC: Powering Progress

PPMC (Power Planning and Monitoring Company) serves as the policy, strategic, and technical arm of the Ministry of Energy (Power Division). We are dedicated to designing and developing comprehensive Electricity Plans for Pakistan.

Our expertise includes carrying out simulations, studies, scenarios, and economic analysis to provide sectorial support on policy directives and interventions envisaged in the National Electricity Plan. We are committed to fostering a sustainable and resilient energy infrastructure.
